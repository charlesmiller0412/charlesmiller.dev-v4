Usability Study and Redesign

As a group, myself and 2 other members of the redesign team conducted a study on the usability of the website for the Dutch Bros Coffee Company. The stages of the process consisted of research, usability testing based on common tasks a user would need to perform, persona creation based on demographics of participants, creating solutions to all issues discovered, and delivering mockups of final solutions. All documentation can be seen by clicking the links below. You can also find a gallery just below the links of the final mockups that were delivered.

The first step in the process consisted of designing the questionnaire that would be given to participants to complete while performing the usability test. This questionnaire included demographical information on the participant for better persona creations, tasks to be completed on the website, rating and responses to the experience while browsing and completing the tasks, and an observation sheet for the test observer to complete while the participant completes the tasks.

Next, we created multiple personas based off of the demographics of the participants that were most common, as well as what the typical customer could be.
