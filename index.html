    I'm Charles and I'm a Front-End Developer in Austin, TX

    I’m a mostly front-end developer from Austin, TX specializing in creating unique and beautiful websites while using easily readable semantic code. From my past experience in the military and the fire service, relocation and adaptability are no hurdle for the right role. The ability to lead when necessary, obsessive attention to detail, overcome stressful situations, solve problems in critical timeframes, and work as a team, is all second nature to me. Have a need for a language not listed on my skills? I’m always eager to learn new skills and happen to pick them up very quickly.

    While most of my time is spent in front of a computer coding away, there is a side of me that you can’t see at work.
